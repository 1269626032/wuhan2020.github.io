Projects

The community has released the following open-sourced projects

Wuhan Novel Coronavirus Outbreak Prevention Information Collection Platform

Github: https://github.com/wuhan2020/wuhan2020

Wuhan novel coronavirus outbreak prevention information collection platform

2020 Wuhan Rescue

Github: https://github.com/wuhan2020/data-sync

Our goal is to establish a real-time data service platform with information on hospitals, factories and procurement, allowing anyone with relevant skills to participate in the initiative. We would like to call for anyone who wishes to contribute to the fight against the 2019-nCoV.

Wuhan2020 Open Source Community Official Website

Github: https://github.com/wuhan2020/wuhan2020.github.io

Wuhan2020 community calls for volunteers all over the world to establish an open-source information platform and contribute to the fight against the 2019-nCoV

2019-nCoV Outbreak Navigation Site

Github: https://github.com/wuhan2020/2019-nCoV-Nav

2019-nCoV navigation site provides the public with a convenient and comprehensive information portal with online resources and archival information related to 2019-nCoV.
